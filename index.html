WrestleMania is WWE's crown jewel — the "Super Bowl of sports entertainment." Every year it brings together the biggest rivalries, championship showdowns, celebrity appearances, and the most unforgettable moments in professional wrestling.


WrestleMania 42 descended on Las Vegas for a two-night spectacle inside Allegiant Stadium, hosted by the iconic John Cena. From CM Punk's redemption arc to Cody Rhodes defending his championship, Night 1 and Night 2 delivered history in the desert.

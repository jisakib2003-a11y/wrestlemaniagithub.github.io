01 — About the Event

The Showcase
of the Immortals

WrestleMania is WWE's crown jewel — the annual extravaganza often dubbed the "Super Bowl of Sports Entertainment." Each year it brings together the biggest rivalries, championship showdowns, celebrity appearances, and the unforgettable story conclusions that fans have waited all year to witness.

WrestleMania 42 returned to the glittering spectacle of Las Vegas, hosted across two electrifying nights at Allegiant Stadium — with John Cena commanding the stage as host in what became a landmark chapter in WWE history.
